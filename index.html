Key Discovery

The Core Reframe
The morning pop isn't the trade. It's the setup. The afternoon pop isn't the trade. It's the setup. You're not trying to catch the move — you're waiting for it to exhaust itself, then fading the reversal off what it left behind. The pop creates your conditions.

The Rule That Changes Everything

4
Trades Per Day. Maximum. No Exceptions.
Not 4 unless 4/4 wins. Not 4 unless it feels right.
4 trades. Session over. P&L irrelevant.

At 75% win rate, the probability of going 4/4 is 31.6% — roughly 1 in 3 days you'll be at 4/4 looking for a reason to take trade 5. That's the rule's most dangerous moment, not its rarest.
Why Dollar Stops Don't Work Here
A dollar stop says you can keep trading until you lose X. That leaves the door open for trades 8, 9, 10 on a good day that turns. 4 trades closes the door regardless of P&L. A perfect 4/4 day is the best possible outcome — bank it and walk away.
